Work I've done

Final Year Project
🌱
IoT-Based Soil Health Monitoring Kit
Siddharth Institute of Engineering & Technology · Guided by Dr. Bogalamadhu, MTech., Ph.D.

Designed and developed a compact, low-cost IoT-based soil health monitoring system capable of measuring real-time soil moisture, temperature, and humidity. The system integrates a soil moisture sensor and DHT11 sensor with a NodeMCU ESP8266 microcontroller, displays live readings on an OLED screen, and transmits data wirelessly via Wi-Fi to the Blynk IoT platform — enabling farmers to remotely monitor field conditions through a mobile or web interface. The kit was successfully tested and validated with consistent sensor readings across both local display and cloud dashboard.
